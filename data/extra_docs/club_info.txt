Committees and departments in the club:
- Program Committee: plans and organizes events, competitions, and workshops
- Media Committee: design, social media posts, and event documentation
- Website Committee: builds and maintains the club website
- Nominations Committee: internal events, team spirit, and IEEE membership promotion
- Execution and Finance Committee: budget management and task execution
